SO UNIQE VEGAN LAB

so unique vegan lab is a small and cozy cafe located in Ansan, S.Korea.
The cafe offers nice coffee with four plant-based milk along with other beverages and vegan desserts, some savory options.
What unique about this cafe is you can find eco-friendly products such as bamboo tooth brush, coconut brush, stainless straws.
